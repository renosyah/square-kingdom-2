cities
- administrative
	- Manor >> Keep >> Castle >> Stronghold (add more building slot) (2,4,6,8)

- military
	- Training field >> Barrack (% cheaper melee equiped unit) (10%, 25%)
	- Practice Range >> Hunting Camp (% cheaper range equiped unit) (10%, 25%)
	- Horse pasture >> Horse Stable (unlock & % cheaper mounted unit) (0%, 15%)
	- Blacksmith >> Weaponsmith (unlock melee weapon) 
	- Bow maker >> Fletcher (unlock range weapon) 
	- Tanner >> Armorer (unlock helm & body armor) 

- farm & industry
	- Market (+100 income - commerce)
	- Farm (+100 income - farm)
	- Mill >> Granary (% income from farm) (25%, 50%)

equipment
	melee weapon :
		- (no requirement) : dagger, pitchfork, spear, axe
		- blacksmith : pike, sword, mace
		- weaponsmith : great sword, great axe, Warhammer

	range weapon : 
		- (no requirement) : javelin, throwing axe
		- bow maker : bow
		- fletcher : crossbow, longbow

	head armor : 
		- (no requirement) : cape, hood
		- Tanner : kettle, helm 1, helm 2, leather armor, medium armor, shields
		- Armorer : helm 3, plate armor
